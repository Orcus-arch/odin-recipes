Chocolate cheesecake

A cool cake for this hot summer!

Ingredients:

  • 4(1 ounce)squares of semisweet chocolate;
  • 2(8ounce) packages cream cheese,softned;
  • 1/2 cup of white sugar;
  • half teaspoon of vanilla extrat;
  • 1(9inch) prepared chocolate cookie crumb crust;
  • 2 eggs;

Preparation:

Step 1

Preheat oven to 175 celsius degree. in the top of a double boiler, heat chocolate,stirring occasionally, until os melted and smooth.Remove from heat and allow to cool to lukewarm.

Step 2

In a large bowl,beat the creams cheese, sugar and vanilla untill smooth.Slowly beat in eggs,one at time.blend in melted chocolate. Pourfilling into crust. ,

Step 3

Bake in the preheated oven for over 40 min,or until filling is set. Allow to cool. refrigerate for at least 3 hours bfore serving.
