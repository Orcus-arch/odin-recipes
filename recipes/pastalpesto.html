Pasta la pesto

A easy italian plate coming from the city of Genova.

ingredients:

  • half cup of chopped onion
  • 3 spoon of pesto
  • 2 spoon of olive oil
  • 2 spoon of grated parmigian chesse
  • 1 package of pasta
  • salt
  • ground black pepper

Preparation:

Step 1:

Cook pasta in a large pot of boiling water, ad a spoon of salt, let it cook auntil done. drain it.

Step 2:

meanwile the pasta is cooking,heat the oil in a frying pan over a medium low heat. add pesto,onion a pinch of salt and pepper. Cook about five min, or until onion are soft.

Step 3:

In a large bowl,mix pesto and pasta. Stir in grated cheese.Serve.
