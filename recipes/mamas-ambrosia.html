Mama's Ambrodsia

A easy and quick fruit salad

ingredients:

  • 2(11 ounce) cans mandarin orange segments drained;
  • 2(15.25 ounce) cans fruit cockyail, drained;
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts;
  • 2 cups miniature mashmallows;
  • 2 cups flacked coconut;
  • 1(8 ounce) container sour cream;

Preparation:

Step 1

In a large bowl, mix toghether the orenges, fruit cocktail, marshmallows walnuts and coconut. Fold in sour cream. Chill for at least for an hour. Stir again before serving.
